164. MAKING EXCEPTIONS IN PRONOUNCEMENTS OF DIVORCE

514. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm concerning a man who said to his wife, “You are thrice divorced inshā’Allāh.”1491 He said, “It is nothing1492 and divorce is not expedited against her.”’”1493

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Connecting [the inshā’Allāh]. ↩
  2. Divorce is not executed. ↩
