162. CHRISTIANS, JEWS AND ZOROASTRIANS WHO DIVORCE THEIR WIVES

509. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning Jews, Christians and Zoroastrians who divorce their wives1481 and then subsequently accept Islam, ‘They remain in their condition of being divorced.1482 Islam only increases them in rigour.’”1483

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

And it is not ḥalāl for them to take them back after accepting Islam since the Messenger of Allah confirmed people in that condition which Islam found them in whether divorce, marriage or inheritance. For example, if a man divorced his wife three times, and they were both Christians or something else, and then both accepted Islam after that, it is not ḥalāl for him to marry her until she has married someone else, in the same way as the judgement is in Islam because of that which ʿAbd ar-Razzāq narrated in his Muṣannaf from Ibn Jurayj, “I said to ʿAṭā’, ‘A man divorced his wife while associating partners with Allah and made it irrevocable, and then they later accepted Islam?’ And he said, ‘I only think that she is not ḥalāl for him until she has married another husband.’” ↩
