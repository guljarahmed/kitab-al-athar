167. THE MAINTENANCE OF A WOMAN WITH WHOM MARRIAGE HAS NOT BEEN CONSUMMATED

519. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm spoke concerning a man who marries a woman but does not consummate the marriage with her and he said, ‘If the self-restraint is on the side of the man, he has to pay maintenance,1502 but if it is on the side of the woman, she has no maintenance.’”1503

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. If she is young, of such an age that the like of her does not have sexual intercourse, she has no maintenance. However, if she is adult and the husband is young, of such an age that the like of him does not have sexual intercourse, then he owes her maintenance from his property. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Because she has submitted but the incapacity is only on his part. ↩
  2. Whether or not she is in the husband’s house until she comes into the condition in which she is able to have sexual intercourse. Ash-Shāfiʿī said that she has maintenance. ↩
