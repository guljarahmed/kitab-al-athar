151. A MAN WHO DIVORCES A SLAVE WOMAN WITH A DIVORCE IN WHICH HE RETAINS THE RIGHT TO TAKE HER BACK

490. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a slave woman is divorced by her husband with him retaining the right to take her back, and then she is set free, her ʿiddah is that of a free woman. But if her husband does not retain the right to take her back, her ʿiddah is that of a slave woman.’”1438

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
