165. A MAN WHO SAYS TO HIS WIFE, “OBSERVE THE ʿIDDAH!”

515. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘“Observe the ʿiddah!” is a divorce in which one retains the right to take her back1494 if he intended divorce.’”1495

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

516. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham ibn Abī’l-Haytham narrated to us, ascribing it to the Messenger of Allah , that he said to Sawdah, “Observe the ʿiddah!” and he made it a single divorce in which he retained [the right to take her back]. So one day, she sat in his path and said, “Messenger of Allah, take me back, for by Allah! I am not saying this out of eagerness on my part1496 for men, but I want to be gathered on the Day of Rising along with your wives, and I will appoint my day with you for one of your wives.”1497 So he took her back.’”1498

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. If a woman herself is contented to remain with her husband on the basis that he does not apportion her [a day], then that is valid, but she may change her mind about that if it occurs to her, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
