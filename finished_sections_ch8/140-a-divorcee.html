140. A DIVORCEE WHO TAKES HIS WIFE BACK; FROM WHERE DOES SHE BEGIN HER ʿIDDAH?

469. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ibrāhīm who said, ‘If a man divorces his wife and does not take her back, but divorces her another time, her ʿiddah is counted from the first of the divorces. However if he divorces her, then takes her back and then divorces her, her ʿiddah starts afresh.’”1383

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
