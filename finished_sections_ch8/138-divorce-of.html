138. DIVORCE OF A SLAVE GIRL WHO HAS NOT MENSTRUATED AND HER ʿIDDAH

466. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a man divorces his wife and she is a girl1372 and she is not menstruating, let her reckon her ʿiddah by months. However, if she then menstruates before the months are over, she should not reckon the ʿiddah by months but by menstrual periods.’”1373 1374

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”

Footnotes

  1. Who has not reached puberty and does not menstruate. ↩
  2. i.e., starting over from the beginning. ↩
  3. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.145. ↩
