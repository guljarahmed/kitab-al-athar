152. DIVORCE OBTAINED BY A WOMAN IN RETURN FOR COMPENSATION PAID TO THE HUSBAND (KHULʿ)

491. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Every divorce for which compensation1439 is accepted is final and the right to take the wife back is not retained.’”1440

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. juʿl is the noun and jaʿl the verbal noun (maṣdar) and means the reward for anything. ↩
