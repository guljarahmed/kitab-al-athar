154. A MAN WHO DIVORCES AND THEN DENIES IT

494. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a woman who heard that her husband had divorced her three times,1447 ‘She opens litigation against him, and if he swears an oath that he did not do it, she ransoms herself1448 [from him] with her own wealth. If he refuses her wealth, she should flee. Then if he has power over her, she should only come to him angered against him1449 and against her will. She should allow herself to become foul smelling1450 and should not deck herself out1451 nor perfume herself.’”1452

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
