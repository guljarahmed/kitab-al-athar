153. IMPOTENCE

492. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning someone who is impotent1441 and has been separated from his wife that it1442 is a final divorce.”1443 1444

493. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ismāʿīl ibn Muslim al-Makkī narrated to us from al-Ḥasan from ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb that a woman came to him and told him that her husband was unable to unite with her [in sexual intercourse] and so he set him a term of a year.1445 When the year had passed and he had not united with her, he gave her the choice and she chose herself. So ʿUmar separated them and made the divorce final.”1446

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
