160. WHAT TYPES OF DIVORCE ARE DEPLORED

506. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm spoke concerning the words of Allah, exalted is He, ‘Do not retain them in such a way as to cause them injury,...’ (Sūrat al-Baqarah: 229) and he said, ‘[It is when] a man divorces once, then leaves her until she has menstruated three times but just before she has completed the third time he says to her, “I take you back,” doing that to her until he has kept her for nine menstrual periods before she is permissible for marriage to men. This is done in such a way as to cause injury.’”

Muḥammad said, “We do not think that he has any right to do this and lengthen the ʿiddah for her.”

507. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm who said, “There is nothing which Allah permits that is more hateful to Allah than divorce.”’”1476

Footnotes

  1. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.148, and Ibn ʿUmar narrated it as a marfūʿ hadith, which Abū Dāwūd narrated under “divorce” in the chapter on “the abhorence of divorce,” and Ibn Mājah narrated under “divorce.” ↩
