146. THE ʿIDDAH OF A WOMAN WITH CHRONIC MENSTRUAL BLEEDING

481. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who divorces his wife while she is suffering from chronic menstrual bleeding, ‘She observes the ʿiddah of the days of her [usual] periods.’1407 He said, ‘It is similar if she begins to have chronic menstrual bleeding after he has divorced her.’”1408

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

482. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘The woman with chronic menstrual bleeding when divorced observes the ʿiddah according to the days of her ordinary periods. Then when she has finished, she is permissible to men [in marriage].’”1409

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
