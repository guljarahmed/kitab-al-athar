28. RECITATION BEHIND THE IMAM AND PROMPTING HIM

84. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said, “When he was behind the imam, ʿAlqamah ibn Qays never recited297 the Umm al-Qur’ān [Fātiḥah] nor anything else in that in which the recitation was aloud nor in that in which the recitation was not aloud, nor in the last two rakʿahs.”’”298

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We do not think299 that one should recite behind the imam in any part of the prayer whether or not he recites aloud.”

85. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said, “Do not do anything extra in the last two rakʿahs over and above the Fātiḥah of the Book.”’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

86. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Abū’l-Ḥasan Mūsā ibn Abī ʿĀ’ishah narrated to us from ʿAbdullāh ibn Shaddād ibn al-Hād300 that Jābir ibn ʿAbdullāh al-Anṣārī said, “The Messenger of Allah prayed301 with a man behind him reciting, so a man from the Companions of the Prophet began to forbid him302 to recite during the prayer. So he said,303 ‘Do you forbid me to recite behind the Prophet of Allah ?’ And they quarrelled until that was mentioned to the Prophet . The Prophet said, ‘Whoever prays behind an imam, the imam’s recitation is his recitation.’”’”304

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

87. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Saʿīd ibn Jubayr said, ‘Recite305 behind the imam in Ẓuhr and ʿAṣr, and do not recite behind him in anything else.’”306

Muḥammad said, “No one should recite behind the imam in any of the prayers.”

88. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning when the imam makes a mistake in an āyah, ‘Let him recite the āyah after that,307 but if he does not do that308 then let him recite another sūrah, and if he does not do that then let him bow in rukūʿ if he had already recited three āyāt or something like that. If he does not do [any of that] then prompt him, but he has done wrong.’”309

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
