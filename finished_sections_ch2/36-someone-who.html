36. SOMEONE WHO PRAYS WITH A WALL OR A PATHWAY BETWEEN HIM AND THE IMAM

114. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Ḥammād said, ‘I asked Ibrāhīm about mu’adhdhins calling people to prayer from on top of the mosque and then praying on top of the mosque. He said, “It is acceptable from them.”’”380

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to that as long as they are not ahead of the imam. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

115. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man between whom and the imam there is a wall, ‘That is fine, as long as there is not a pathway381 or women382 between him and the imam.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
