43. WHAT INVALIDATES THE PRAYER

133. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If the imam’s prayer is invalid then the prayer of those behind him is invalid.’”431

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. If a man in need of a ghusl because of sexual relations or without wuḍū’ prays or if his prayer is invalid for any reason whatsoever, then the prayer of those behind him is invalid.”

134. Muḥammad said, “Ibrāhīm ibn Yazīd al-Makkī informed us from ʿAmr ibn Dīnār that ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib said concerning the man who leads people in prayer while in need of a ghusl because of sexual relations, ‘He must repeat432 the prayer and so must they.’”

135. Muḥammad from433 ʿAbdullāh ibn al-Mubārak from Yaʿqūb ibn al-Qaʿqāʿ that ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ said concerning a man who led his companions in prayer without being in wuḍū’, “He must repeat the prayer and so must they.”434

136. Muḥammad said, “ʿAbdullāh ibn al-Mubārak informed us from ʿAbdullāh ibn ʿAwn that Muḥammad ibn Sīrīn said, ‘We prefer435 that they repeat the prayer.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

137. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘When a woman prays beside a man436 in the same prayer, his prayer is invalid.’”437

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

138. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from ʿĀ’ishah that the Prophet used to pray while she slept by his side438 and he was wearing a garment439 one side of which lay over her.”440

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, and we see no harm in that. It is similar if she prayed by his side but performing another prayer than the one he was performing. It only invalidates his prayer if she is beside him and they are performing the same prayer, with him acting as her imam441 or both of them following another imam. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

139. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Ḥammād said, ‘I asked Ibrāhīm about a man praying in the eastern side of the mosque and a woman praying in the western side and he disapproved of that unless between them there was442 something that amounted to the rear part of the camel saddle.’”443

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, when they are in the same prayer praying with a single imam.”

140. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that al-Aswad ibn Yazīd asked ʿĀ’ishah the Mother of the Mu’minūn about what interrupts the prayer [and invalidates it by passing in front of it], and she said, ‘Do you not claim, people of Iraq,444 that donkeys, dogs, women and cats445 interrupt the prayer, and so you couple us [women] with them [animals]? Avert what you can, because nothing interrupts446 the prayer.’”447

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to the verdict of ʿĀ’ishah and it is verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

141. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb said, ‘The ugliest448 vice is any discourse after the prayer of ʿIshā’ except for in prayer449 or recitation of Qur’ān.’”
