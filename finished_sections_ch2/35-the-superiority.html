35. THE SUPERIORITY OF THE GROUP PRAYER AND OF THE TWO RAKʿAHS OF FAJR

109. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There are four before Ẓuhr and four after the Jumuʿah which one does not separate with a salām.’”369

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

110. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Saʿīd ibn Jubayr said, ‘A man’s prayer with the group is twenty-five370 prayers better than a man’s prayer alone.’”

111. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Ḥārith ibn Ziyād or Muḥārib ibn Dithār’ (Muḥammad being in doubt371 as to which) ‘narrated to us that ʿAbdullāh ibn ʿUmar said, “Whoever prays four rakʿahs after ʿIshā’ before leaving the mosque, then they are equal372 to four rakʿahs on Laylat al-Qadr.”’”

112. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAlqamah ibn Marthad narrated to us from ʿAlī [ibn al-Aqmar] from Ḥumrān. [ʿAlī] said, “Ibn ʿUmar was never to be seen narrating but that Ḥumrān was one of the closest people to him in the assembly.” [ʿAlī] said, “So he said to him one day, ‘I think373 that you cling so close to us so that we can bring you some good.’ He said, ‘Of course, Abū ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān.’374 He said, ‘Watch out375 for three things, two of which I forbid you and one of which I command you to do.’ He asked, ‘What are they Abū ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān?’ He said, ‘Do not die owing a debt except a debt for which you leave payment.376 Never repudiate a child of yours, because in retaliation he will make you notorious for it on the day of Rising just as you gave him notoriety377 in the world. Your Lord does not wrong anyone. Take care of the two rakʿahs of Fajr and do not give them up because they are of the highly desired matters.’”’”

113. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Maʿn ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān narrated to us from al-Qāsim ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān from his father that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, ‘Treat the prayer with dignity,’378 meaning be still in it.”379

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
