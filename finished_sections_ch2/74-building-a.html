74. BUILDING A MOUND799 OVER THE GRAVE AND PLASTERING IT WITH GYPSUM

255. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Someone informed me that they had seen the grave of the Prophet , the grave of Abū Bakr , and the grave of ʿUmar with mounds on top of them protruding prominently from the ground, and on them pieces800 of white clay.’”801

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, the grave is marked with a prominent mound, but it is not to be made in the form of a square,802 and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

256. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘It used to be said803 that one should raise the grave so that it will be recognised as a grave and not stepped on.’”804

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, but we do not think that anything should be added805 to what comes out of it [the soil which comes out of the grave], and we dislike it being plastered with gypsum or with clay, or that a mosque806 or any marker should be built by it or that anything should be written on it. It is disliked that it should be built with baked bricks807 or that they should be put in the interior of the grave. We see no harm808 in sprinkling water on it. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

257. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘A Shaykh of ours809 narrated to us, transmitting it [as a marfūʿ hadith] from the Prophet , that he prohibited building the grave with four sides and plastering it with gypsum.’”810

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

258. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd used to say, “I would rather step on811 a live coal than deliberately step on a grave.”’”812

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. It is disapproved that one should deliberately step on graves, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
