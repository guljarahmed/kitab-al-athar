72. THE PRAYER OVER DECEASED MEN AND WOMEN

245. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a number of funerals that occur at the same time, ‘They [the bodies] are laid in a row, one in front of the other,776 and they are all laid in a single row777 with the imam standing in the middle of it. If there are both men and women, the men are placed nearer to the imam and women in front of that, closer to the qiblah, just in the same way as the men are nearer to the imam in the prayer and the women are behind them.’”778

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

246. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Sulaymān ash-Shaybānī that ʿĀmir ash-Shaʿbī said, ‘Ibn ʿUmar prayed over Umm Kulthūm, the daughter of ʿAlī , and her son, Zayd ibn ʿUmar, and he placed Umm Kulthūm towards the qiblah and Zayd nearer to the imam.’”779

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

247. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿIsa ibn ʿAbdullāh ibn Mawhab narrated to us saying, “I saw Abū Hurayrah praying the funeral prayer over men and women, and he placed the men closer to himself and the women closer to the qiblah.”’”

248. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham narrated to us from Saʿīd ibn ʿAmr that Ibn ʿUmar prayed over a woman who had given birth to an illegitimate child and both of them had died, and so Ibn ʿUmar prayed over her.’”780

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. None of the people of the qiblah should be left without being prayed over, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
