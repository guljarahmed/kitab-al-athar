51. SOMEONE WHO GREETS PEOPLE DURING THE KHUTBAH OR THE PRAYER

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

180. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm564 said, ‘One returns the greeting and asks for blessings565 on the person who sneezes while the imam is delivering the khuṭbah on the day of Jumuʿah.’”566

Muḥammad said, “We do not adhere to this,567 but we adhere to the verdict of Saʿīd ibn al-Musayyab, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

181. Muḥammad said, “Sufyān ibn ʿUyaynah informed us that ʿAbdullāh ibn Saʿīd ibn Abī Hind said, ‘I said to Saʿīd ibn al-Musayyab, “So-and-so sneezed while the imam was delivering the khuṭbah and so-and-so asked for mercy for him.” He said, “Tell him he must not do it again.”’”568

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The khuṭbah has the same rank as the prayer.569 During it one does not ask for blessings on the person who sneezes570 nor does one return greetings.571 That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

182. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he said572 concerning someone who goes to visit his friend and greets him while he is praying, ‘Does he not say573 in the tashahhud, “Peace be upon us and upon the right-acting slaves of Allah”? So he has already returned his greeting.’”574

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. It does not please us that someone should return the greeting while praying, nor that someone should greet a person who is praying, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

183. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who sits behind the imam for the length of the tashahhud and then finishes before the imam, ‘It is not valid for him,’575 but ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ said, ‘If he sits for the length of the tashahhud it is valid for him.’576 Abū Ḥanīfah said, ‘My verdict is the verdict of ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ.’”

Muḥammad said, “We also adhere to the verdict of ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ.”

184. Muḥammad said, “Shuʿbah ibn al-Ḥajjāj informed us that Abū’n-Naḍr said, ‘I heard Ḥamalah ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān saying, “I heard ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb saying, ‘There is no prayer without a tashahhud.’”’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. Then when someone says the tashahhud they have done the prayer, and if they finish before saying the salām that is valid for them, but they ought not to do that577 deliberately.”
