68. TAKING A GHUSL BECAUSE OF HAVING WASHING SOMEONE DECEASED

232. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning the person who had washed a dead person himself taking a ghusl, ‘ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd used to say, “If your [deceased] companion was impure,739 then take a ghusl because of him, but wuḍū’ is sufficient.”’”740

Muḥammad said, “If one wishes also, one need not do wuḍū’. If some of the water with which one washes the deceased falls on one, then one washes it off.741 That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

233. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib used to tell someone who washed the dead to take a ghusl.’”742

Muḥammad said, “We don’t think he told them to do that because he thought it obligatory.”743

234. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who is present at a funeral without wuḍū’, ‘He should do tayammum with pure dust of the earth744 and then pray, but a woman should not do that if she is in her period.’”745 746

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
