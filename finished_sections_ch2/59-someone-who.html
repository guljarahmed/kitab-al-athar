59. SOMEONE WHO EATS BEFORE GOING OUT TO THE PLACE OF PRAYER (MUṢALLĀ)

206. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that it used to please652 Ibrāhīm to eat something653 before going to the place of prayer, meaning on the day of al-Fiṭr.”

207. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm used to eat on the day of al-Fiṭr before going out, but he would not eat on the day of al-Aḍḥā654 until he returned.”655

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. It is recommended. ↩
  2. Something sweet, as is firmly established in the hadith. ↩
  3. It is recommended that the first thing one eats be from the meat of the sacrifices, which are Allah’s hospitality, but if one eats before the prayer it is not disliked. ↩
  4. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.59. ↩
