62. THE QUNŪT664 SUPPLICATION IN PRAYER

211. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Ibn Masʿūd665 used to perform the qunūt throughout the entire year666 in the witr before bowing.”667

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

212. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that the qunūt before bowing during the witr is incumbent668 in the month of Ramaḍān and at other times.669 When you wish to say the qunūt then say the takbīr,670 and when you wish to bow then say the takbīr also.”671

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. One raises one’s hands in the first saying of the takbīr before the qunūt just as one does in beginning the prayer, and then one places them and supplicates. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

213. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Ibn Masʿūd did not say the qunūt,672 neither he nor any of his companions, until he left the world, meaning not in the prayer of Fajr.”673 674

214. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Aṣ-Ṣalt ibn Bahrām narrated to us from Abū’sh-Shaʿthā’675 that Ibn ʿUmar said, “Is that which has reached us about your imam true, that he stands in prayer676 without reciting Qur’ān and without bowing?”’”

Muḥammad said, “Ibn ʿUmar meant by677 that the qunūt in the prayer of Fajr.”

215. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm, ‘The Prophet was not seen saying the qunūt678 in Fajr until he left the world except for one month in which he said the qunūt supplicating against a tribe of those who associate partners with Allah; he was not seen saying qunūt before it nor after it. And Abū Bakr was not seen saying qunūt after him until he left the world.’”679

216. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that al-Aswad ibn Yazīd accompanied ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb for two years, both while travelling and while resident, and he did not see him saying qunūt in Fajr until he left him. Ibrāhīm said, ‘The people of Kufa only learnt the qunūt from ʿAlī who said the qunūt supplicating against Muʿāwiyah when he waged war on him. As for the people of Shām, they only learnt the qunūt from Muʿāwiyah who said the qunūt supplicating against ʿAlī when he waged war on him.’”680

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to the verdict of Ibrāhīm,681 and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
