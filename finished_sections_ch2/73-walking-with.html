73. WALKING WITH THE FUNERAL

249. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Ḥammād said, ‘I saw Ibrāhīm going in front of781the funeral at some distance without disappearing from sight.’”782 783

Muḥammad said, “We see no harm784 in going in front of the bier if one is close to it, but walking behind it is better,785 and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

250. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘It is disapproved786 for someone who is mounted to go ahead of the funeral.’”787

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

251. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Ḥammād said, ‘I asked Ibrāhīm about walking in front of the deceased, and he said, “Walk wherever you wish.788 All that is disliked is that the people set off and sit at the grave abandoning the deceased.”’”789

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

252. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said, “I would sit with the companions of ʿAbdullāh (ibn Masʿūd ): ʿAlqamah, al-Aswad and others, and a funeral would pass by them while they were sitting on their haunches with a cloth bound around their legs and their backs,790 and none of them would loosen his cloth [and stand up].”’”791

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We do not think that anyone should stand up for a bier, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

253. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Ḥammād said, ‘I asked Ibrāhīm, “When do the people sit down?” and he said, “When the deceased is taken down off the men’s shoulders.” He said, “What do you think if they reach the grave and no one has begun to use the pickaxe; would you stand until the grave is dug?”’”792 793

Muḥammad said, “When the bier is placed on the ground there is no harm in sitting down,794 but before that795 it is disliked, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

254. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that al-Ḥārith ibn Abī Rabīʿah’s Christian mother died and he followed her bier with a group of the Companions of the Prophet .”796

Muḥammad said, “We see no harm797 in following her but he should remain aside at a distance798 from the bier, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
