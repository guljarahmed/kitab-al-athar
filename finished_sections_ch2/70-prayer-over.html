70. PRAYER OVER THE DECEASED

236. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is no recitation751 in the funeral prayer, nor bowing nor prostration, but one should say the salām to the right and to the left when one has finished saying the takbīr.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

237. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is nothing specific752 to be said in the prayer over the dead, but begin753 and praise Allah,754 ask for blessings on the Prophet 755 and make supplication756 for yourself and for the deceased with whatever you wish.’”

238. Muḥammad said, “Sufyān ath-Thawrī informed us from Abū Hāshim757 that Ibrāhīm an-Nakhaʿī said, ‘The first thing [after the first takbīr] is praise of Allah, the second [after the second takbīr] is asking for blessings on the Prophet , the third [after the third takbīr] is supplication for the deceased, and the fourth [after the fourth takbīr] is the salām which you say.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

239. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning prayer over the dead, ‘The imams of the mosques758 pray over them.’ Ibrahim said, ‘You are content with them759 for your obligatory prayers and you are not content with them for your dead?’”760

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The legal guardian761 ought to make the imam of the mosque step forward [to lead the prayer], but he is not compelled to do that.762 That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

240. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm, ‘They used763 to say takbīr over the dead five, six764 and four times until the Prophet died. Then they continued like that under the governance of Abū Bakr until Abū Bakr died. Then ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb was appointed and they did that during his governance. When ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb saw that,765 he said, “Assembly of the Companions of Muḥammad if you differ, those who come after you will differ, and people have only newly come out of ignorance,766 so agree upon something which those who come after you can agree upon.” So the Companions of Muḥammad agreed that they should look at the very last funeral767 over which the Prophet had said takbīr at around the time he died and they would adhere to that and reject everything else. So they investigated that and discovered that in the last funeral over which the Messenger of Allah had said takbīr, he had done so four times.’”768

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

241. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham narrated to us from Abū Yaḥyā ʿUmayr ibn Saʿīd an-Nakhaʿī that ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib led the prayer over Yazīd ibn al-Mukaffif, and that he said takbīr four times, and that it was the last time that ʿAlī said takbīr over someone deceased.’”769

242. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Saʿīd ibn al-Marzubān narrated to us that ʿAbdullāh ibn Abī Awfā said takbīr four times over a daughter of his.’”
