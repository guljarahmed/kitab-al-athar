67. WASHING A [DECEASED] WOMAN AND SHROUDING HER

229. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a woman who dies among a group of men,730 ‘Her husband should wash her,731 and similarly if a man dies among a group of women, his wife should wash him.’732 Abū Ḥanīfah said, ‘It is not permitted for a man to wash his deceased wife.’”733 734

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah; the man does not have to observe the ʿiddah period [on being widowed], so how could he wash his wife when it is permissible for him to marry her sister, or even to marry her daughter if he had not consummated the marriage with her mother?”

230. “It has reached us that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb said,735 ‘We had more right to her when she was alive, but as for when she died, you have more right to her.’”736

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”

231. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm concerning shrouding a woman, ‘If you wish, three pieces of cloth,737 and if you wish, four. If you wish, an even number, and if you wish, an odd number.’”738

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
