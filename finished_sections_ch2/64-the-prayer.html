64. THE PRAYER OF SLAVE WOMEN

219. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning slave women, ‘They pray without head-covering or veil,689 even if they reach a hundred years and have children by their owner.’”690

220. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb used to beat slave women691 if they covered their heads, saying, ‘Do not try to emulate free women.’”692

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We do not think693 that slave women should wear head-covering in prayer or at any other time, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

221. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a woman who is praying and wants some everyday need,694 ‘Her answer is to clap her hands.’”695

Muḥammad said, “And her leaving that out is preferable to us.”696
