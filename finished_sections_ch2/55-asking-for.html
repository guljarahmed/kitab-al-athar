55. ASKING FOR BLESSINGS621 ON SOMEONE WHO SNEEZES

198. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a man sneezes and says, “Praise be to Allah,” then say, “May Allah have mercy on us and on you,”622 and let the one who sneezed say, “May Allah forgive us and you.”’”623

Footnotes

  1. It is the response to the person who sneezes and its meaning is to supplicate for blessings and good for him. ↩
  2. And in another narration: “yarḥamuka’llāh” (may Allah have mercy on you). ↩
  3. There has also been narrated, “May Allah guide you and put right your state.” Whichever one says it is valid, and one discharges by that the rights of one’s Muslim brother. ↩
