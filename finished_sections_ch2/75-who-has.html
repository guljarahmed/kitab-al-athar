75. WHO HAS MORE RIGHT TO LEAD THE PRAYER OVER THE DECEASED

259 & 260. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm, and also from ʿAwn ibn ʿAbdullāh813 from ash-Shaʿbī that they both [Ibrāhīm and ash-Shaʿbī] said, ‘The husband has more right to lead the prayer over the deceased [wife] than the father.’”814

261. Abū Ḥanīfah said, “A man informed me from al-Ḥasan that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb said, ‘The father has more right815 to lead the prayer over the deceased than the husband.’”816

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, and Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, used to adhere to it.”
