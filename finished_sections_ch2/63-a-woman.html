63. A WOMAN LEADING OTHER WOMEN IN PRAYER, AND HOW A WOMAN SITS IN PRAYER

217. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn used to lead women682 in prayer during the month of Ramaḍān683 and she would stand in the middle [of the row].’”684

Muḥammad said, “It does not please us685 that a woman should lead the prayer, but if she does she should stand in the middle of the row686 with the women as ʿĀ’ishah did. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

218. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning women sitting in the prayer, ‘They sit however they wish.’”687 688

Muḥammad said, “It is preferable to us that she should gather her legs to one side, and not set [her foot] erect as do men.”
