69. CARRYING THE BIER

235. Muḥammad from Abū Ḥanīfah who said, “Manṣūr ibn al-Muʿtamir narrated to us from Sālim ibn Abī’l-Jaʿd from ʿUbayd ibn Nusṭās that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, ‘It is from the Sunnah747 to carry the bier by the four748 sides of the stretcher, and anything beyond that is a voluntary extra act.’”749 750

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The man begins by placing the right fore part of the deceased to his right, and then he places the right rear part of the deceased to his right. Then he returns to the left fore part and places it to his left, and then comes to the left rear part and places it to his left, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. The Sunnah of the Messenger of Allah . ↩
  2. Four men lift it up on their upper backs and shoulders, not on their necks, each one of the four carrying it and walking ten steps with it because of the hadith, “Whoever carries a bier for forty steps it will expiate forty major wrong actions for him.” ↩
  3. An additional extra. It is a due upon each Muslim that he carries it one time by each of its four corners for ten steps. If he does it more than that and carries it more times, then that is an increase in reward and a supererogatory act for him. If he does not carry it again after that, he will not be taken to task for it. ↩
  4. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.81. ↩
