79. RECITATION OF THE QUR’ĀN

270. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Yaḥyā ibn ʿAmr ibn Salamah narrated to us from his father that Ibn Masʿūd said, “Whoever of you recites the three āyāt which are at the end of Sūrat al-Baqarah842 in a night843 has done a lot and done well.”’”844 845

271. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, “Do not read the Qur’ān rapidly846 and uninterruptedly as one does with poetry, and do not scatter it like scattering poor quality dates.”’”847 848

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The reciter ought to understand what he is reciting, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

272. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿĀṣim ibn Abī’n-Najūd narrated to us from Abū’l-Aḥwaṣ that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd , “Certainly, for every letter which the reciter recites, ten good actions [are recorded for him]. Certainly, I do not say to you that Alif Lām Mīm is a letter,849 but rather that Alif and Lām and Mīm are thirty good actions [recorded for you].”’”850

273. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘A man ought not to shift from one recitation851 to another.’”852

Muḥammad said, “Meaning the mode of recitation of ʿAbdullāh853 and the mode of recitation of Zayd854 and others.”

274. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Ibn Masʿūd would teach a non-Arab man,855 ‘The Tree of az-Zaqqūm856 is the food of the wicked (al-athīm),’ (Sūrat ad-Dukhān: 41) but if he was incapable of reciting it, ʿAbdullāh would say to him, ‘Can you not recite well,857 “...the food of the fājir (a synonym also meaning wicked)?’” ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, ‘Making a mistake in the Book of Allah is not when you recite some of it in place of another,858 saying [for example], “The All-Forgiving859 the All-Merciful or the All-Forgiving the All-Wise, and the All-Mighty the All-Wise or the All-Mighty the All-Merciful,” for Allah, blessed is He and exalted, is such.860 But what is a mistake is that you recite an āyah concerning punishment as an āyah of mercy, or an āyah of mercy as an āyah of punishment, or that you add to the Book of Allah what is not in it.’”861

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

275. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb used to say, “Beautify862 your voices with the Qur’ān.”’”863

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. Recitation in our view is as Ṭāwus narrated. He said, ‘Among the best of people in recitation is the person who when you hear him recite, you think that he fears Allah.’”

276. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘It used to be said,864 “Allah, blessed is He and exalted, does not listen to anything as He listens to the Qur’ān [recited] with a beautiful voice.”’”865
