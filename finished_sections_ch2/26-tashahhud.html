26. TASHAHHUD

78. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Bilāl278 narrated to us from Wahb ibn Kaysān that Jābir ibn ʿAbdullāh al-Anṣārī said, “The Messenger of Allah used to teach us the tashahhud and the saying of takbīr279 in the prayer just as he would teach us a sūrah of the Qur’ān.”’”280

79. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he [Ḥammād] said, ‘I said,281 “Should I say, ‘In the name of Allah’?”282 but he [Ibrāhīm] said, “Say, ‘Greetings are for Allah.’”’”283

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We do not think284 that even a letter should be added into the tashahhud or taken away from it, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

80. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘They used to say the tashahhud285 at the time of the Messenger of Allah and say in their tashahhud, “Peace be upon Allah,”286 and so the Prophet finished one day, turned to face them, and said to them, “Do not say, ‘Peace be upon Allah.’ Allah is Peace. Rather say, ‘Peace be upon us and upon the right-acting slaves of Allah.’”’”287

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
