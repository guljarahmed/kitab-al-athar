78. VISITING GRAVES

269. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAlqamah ibn Marthad narrated to us from Ibn Buraydah al-Aslamī from his father [Buraydah] that the Prophet said, “We forbade you to visit graves, but now visit834 them and do not use bad language,835 for permission has been granted to Muḥammad836 to visit the grave of his mother. [We forbade you] to retain the meat of [ʿĪd] sacrifices837 for more than three days, but now retain as much as you wish and take provision from it, because we only forbade you so that those of you who are wealthy would be generous to those of you who are poor. [We forbade you to drink] pressed juice838 which had been left to stand [nabīdh] in gourds,839 or in green glazed wine-jars and skins or vessels smeared with pitch, but now prepare pressed juice in any receptacle because a receptacle does not make anything ḥalāl or ḥarām. However, do not drink intoxicants.840”’”841

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this. There is no harm in visiting graves for supplication for the dead and in order to remember the next life, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
