66. FUNERALS711 AND WASHING THE DEAD

223. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘The deceased is washed an odd number of times: twice with water and one time with lote-tree leaves,712 and that should be in the middle. [The deceased’s clothing and the bier] are fumigated with incense an odd number of times, and his last travelling provision for the grave should not be713 that he is followed by fire. His shrouding should be done with an odd number714 of cloths.’”715

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this except in one respect: if you wish, make the shrouding an odd number, or if you wish, make it an even number.”

224. “It has reached us that Abū Bakr aṣ-Ṣiddīq said, ‘Wash these two garments of mine and shroud me in them,’ and this was an even number,716 and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”717

225. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿĀṣim ibn Sulaymān narrated to us from Ibn Sīrīn from Ibn ʿUmar that [Ibn Sīrīn] said, “[A man] asked him [Ibn ʿUmar]718 about musk being put in the mix of perfumes for the deceased, and he said, ‘Is it not one of the most fragrant of your perfumes?’”’”719

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”720

226. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he [Ḥammād] said, ‘It used to be deplored721 to put saffron or wars722 in the mix of perfumes for the deceased,723 and he said, “Put whatever fragrance you wish in it.”’”724

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”

227. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn , saw a corpse725 with its hair combed726 and she said, ‘Why do you take your deceased by the forelock?’”727 728

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We do not think that the head of the deceased should be combed nor any of the hair cut, nor should the nails be clipped. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

228. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that the Prophet was shrouded in a Yemeni garment and shirt.”729

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. We think that a man should be shrouded in three cloths but two are sufficient. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
