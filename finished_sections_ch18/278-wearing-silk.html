CLOTHING

278. WEARING SILK, DRESSING OSTENTATIOUSLY, AND FABRICS WOVEN OF BOTH WOOL AND SILK

846. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb dispatched an army. Allah gave them victory and they gained much booty. When they returned and it reached ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb that they were approaching, he went out along with people to receive them. When it reached them that ʿUmar had come out with people, they dressed themselves in the silk and brocade they had. When ʿUmar saw them he was angry and turned away from them and said, ‘Take off the dress of the people of the Fire!’ When they saw ʿUmar’s anger , they took it off. Then they came and offered their excuses and said, ‘We dressed in it to show you the booty which Allah restored to us.’ That lifted [the anger]2132 from ʿUmar and he granted a concession for wearing [the amount of] a finger or two, or three or four.”2133

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

847. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, ‘Beware of two ostentations in dress, that any of you becomes so humble that he wears wool,2134 or he carries himself with such a haughty and conceited manner that he dresses in silk.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

848. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us that Sulaymān ibn Abī al-Mughīrah said, ‘While I was sitting with him Yaḥyā asked Saʿīd ibn Jubayr about dressing in silk. Saʿīd said, “Ḥudhayfah ibn al-Yamān was absent for a period and his son and daughters clothed themselves in shirts of silk. When he returned he ordered that they be taken off the males and left on the females.”’”2135

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

849. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham ibn Abī’l-Haytham al-Baṣrī narrated to us that ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān, ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān ibn ʿAwf, Abū Hurayrah, Anas ibn Mālik,ʿImrān ibn Ḥuṣayn, and Ḥusayn as well as Shurayḥ used to wear fabric woven from wool and raw-silk (khazz).’”2136 2137

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

850. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Saʿīd ibn al-Marzubān narrated to us that ʿAbdullāh ibn Abī Awfā used to wear fabric woven from wool and raw-silk (khazz).’”2138

851. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Zayd ibn Abī Anīsah narrated to us from a man2139 of the people of Egypt that the Prophet took hold of silk and gold in his hand said, “This is forbidden to the males of my community.”’”2140

Muḥammad said, “We see no harm in it for women, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

852. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is no harm in silk and gold for women.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

853. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from ʿAmr ibn Dīnār that ʿĀ’ishah decked her sisters with gold jewellery and that Ibn ʿUmar decked his daughters with gold jewellery.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
