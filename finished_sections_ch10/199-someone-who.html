199. SOMEONE WHO HAS SEXUAL INTERCOURSE BECAUSE OF A MISTAKEN UNDERSTANDING

619. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿAlqamah was asked about someone’s wife’s slave girl1706 and he said, ‘I do not care if you have intercourse with her or with the slave girl of ʿAwsajah.’”1707 He said, “ʿAwsajah was the superintendent1708 of his area.”1709 1710

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict: someone’s wife’s slave girl and all others are the same [they are equally forbidden], except that if he had intercourse with her because of a misunderstanding [of her legal position] we would avert from him the ḥadd punishment. Thus it has reached us from ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib and Ibn Masʿūd .”

620. Muḥammad said, “Sufyān ath-Thawrī informed us from al-Mughīrah aḍ-Ḍabbī from al-Haytham ibn Badr from Ḥurqūṣ1711 from ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib that a woman came to ʿAlī and said, ‘My husband had sexual intercourse with my slave woman,’ and he [her husband] said, ‘She1712 has told the truth. She and her property are mine.’ He [ʿAlī] said, ‘Go away, and do not do it again.’”1713

Muḥammad said, “The punishment is averted from him since it is an ambiguity.”
