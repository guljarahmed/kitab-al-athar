192. DISCRETIONARY PUNISHMENTS (TAʿZĪR)

609. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham ibn Abī’l-Haytham narrated to us from ʿĀmir ash-Shaʿbī who said, “Discretionary punishments must not amount to forty1684 lashes.”’”1685

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict.”

610. Muḥammad said, “Misʿar ibn Kidām informed us saying, ‘Al-Walīd ibn ʿUthmān informed me from aḍ-Ḍaḥḥāk ibn Mazāḥim who said, “The Messenger of Allah said, ‘Whoever reaches the [amount of the] ḥadd punishment for something other than a ḥadd is one of the transgressors.’”’”

Muḥammad said, “The least ḥadd punishment is forty lashes, so discretionary punishments must not amount to forty lashes.”
