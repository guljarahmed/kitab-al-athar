197. THE PUNISHMENT FOR THE SODOMITE1694

616. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm who said, “The sodomite has the same status as the adulterer.”’”1695 1696

Muḥammad said, “This is our verdict. If he is or has been married,1697 he is stoned. If he is not or has not been married, he is flogged one hundred times as a ḥadd punishment.”

617. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād who said, ‘Whoever slanders someone with an unsubstantiated accusation of sodomy is flogged as a ḥadd punishment.’”1698

Muḥammad said, “That is our verdict1699 if he is explicit and is not alluding. As for if he says [to another man], ‘O Lūṭī!’then this1700 may have another origin1701 which is not an unsubstantiated accusation of sexual impropriety,1702 and so we do not give him a ḥadd punishment unless he is explicit.”

In az-Ziyādāt there is that it is up to whatever the imam (sultan) thinks, and he may have him killed if he wishes or have him beaten and imprisoned if he wishes. ↩
