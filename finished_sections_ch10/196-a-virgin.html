196. A VIRGIN MAN WHO FORNICATES WITH A VIRGIN WOMAN

614. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from Ibn Masʿūd who said concerning a virgin man who fornicated with a virgin woman, ‘They are both flogged and exiled for a year1691’ ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib said, ‘Exiling them is an ordeal.’”1692 1693

615. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Exile is sufficient ordeal.’”

Muḥammad said, “So I asked Abū Ḥanīfah, ‘What did Ibrāhīm mean by saying, “Exile is sufficient ordeal”? That he should not be exiled?’ He said, ‘Yes.’”

Muḥammad said, “And this is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict. We adhere to the verdict of ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib .”

Footnotes

  1. This was ash-Shāfiʿī’s verdict that they are given both of these as a ḥadd punishment, but according to us a virgin is not given both a flogging and exile as a ḥadd punishment. Exile and expulsion are not legislated as a ḥadd punishment but as a politic handling of the situation and as a discretionary punishment as ʿUmar said concerning the events surrounding Rabīʿah ibn Umayyah, “I will not exile another Muslim after him,” which is a general statement comprising everyone who perpetrates a wrong action necessitating a ḥadd punishment. ↩
  2. For example, if they were to leave Islam in the lands of the kuffār which would also be an ordeal for Islam and the Muslims. ↩
  3. See the traditions about that in the Muṣannaf of Ibn Abī Shaybah, vol.5, p.536. ↩
