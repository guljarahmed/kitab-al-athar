194. RAPE OF WOMEN

612. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that whoever rapes a woman, whether he is free or a slave, is due the ḥadd punishment1688 and she is not owed a dowry. He said, ‘If the dowry is due, then punishment is averted, and if he is flogged, the dowry is invalid.’”

Muḥammad said, “All of this is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict.”

Footnotes

  1. If illegal sexual intercourse is established, but if that punishment is averted from him because of the existence of some ambiguity then he owes the dowry, and both ḥadd punishment and dowry are not incumbent for a single act of sexual intercourse. ↩
