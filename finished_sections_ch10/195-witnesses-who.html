195. WITNESSES WHO TESTIFY TO A WOMAN’S ADULTERY, ONE OF WHOM IS HER HUSBAND

613. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If four people testify to [a woman’s] adultery one of whom is her husband,1689 the punishment is carried out against her. If they testify and one of them is her husband, and if her husband had consummated the marriage with her, she is stoned. Their testimony is accepted if they fulfil the criteria of being reliable witnesses (ʿudūl).’”1690

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict. If her husband had consummated the marriage with her, she is stoned. If he had not consummated the marriage with her, she is to be flogged with a hundred lashes.”
