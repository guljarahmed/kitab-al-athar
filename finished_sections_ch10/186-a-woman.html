ḤADD PUNISHMENTS

186. A WOMAN WHO RENEGES ON ISLAM

591. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from ʿĀṣim ibn Abī’n-Najūd from Abū Razīn from Ibn ʿAbbās who said, ‘Women are not to be killed when they renege on Islam but they are compelled to return to it.’”1648

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. However, we detain her in prison1649 until she turns in tawbah or dies, except for a slave woman whose owners are sorely in need of her services whom we compel to return to Islam, and who, if she refuses, we give her over to her owners who put her to work and compel her to return to Islam. If someone should kill a woman who reneges and she is a free woman or a slave woman, then he is responsible for nothing in terms of [the free woman’s] compensatory payment or her [the slave woman’s] price, but we deplore that act of his, and if the ruler wishes to have him disciplined he may do so. And it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

592. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘A woman who reneges from Islam is to be killed.’”1650 1651

Muḥammad said, “We do not adhere to this.”

  2. Because she has refused to fulfil the right of Allah, exalted is He, after her affirmation of it and so is compelled to fulfil it by being imprisoned as is in Ḥuqūq al-ʿibād. ↩
  3. Ash-Shāfiʿī gave this verdict because of his saying , “Whoever changes his dīn, kill him,” in which the word “whoever” encompasses men and women. We [Ḥanafīs] have that the Prophet forbade women to be killed. If his saying, “Whoever changes his dīn...” is general (ʿāmm) nevertheless it has to be taken as restricted to men in particular (khāṣṣ) because when a general and particular ruling occur in some ruling then the particular has to qualify the general ruling. When a Muslim man reneges on Islam, whether he is free or a slave, then Islam is offered to him and if he has some misunderstanding it is clarified for him and he is detained for three days. Then if he submits in Islam that is well and good, but otherwise he is killed. ↩
