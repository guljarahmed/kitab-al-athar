193. PUNISHMENTS FOR CONTRAVENTION OF THE LIMITS WHICH WHEN GATHERED TOGETHER INCLUDE AMONG THEM CAPITAL PUNISHMENT

611. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a number of punishments are collected together against one man among which there is execution, the punishments are averted and he is killed.1686 If punishments are collected together1687 and he had killed someone, then he is killed and everything other than that is averted because the execution encompassed all of that.’”

Muḥammad said, “This is all the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah and our verdict except for in the case of the punishment with respect to accusations of adultery and fornication because they are people’s rights. In that case he is flogged for slander and then he is killed. What are averted are only punishments [executed] for the sake of Allah, exalted is He.”

Footnotes

  1. Because there is no need of any other type of chiding and restraint along with that. ↩
  2. Meaning that if he had fornicated, stolen and drunk alcohol then all of their punishments would be executed on him unless among his punishments one was a capital punishment, meaning that if he had killed someone along with fornicating then it is sufficient to execute him. ↩
