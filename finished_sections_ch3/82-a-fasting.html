82. A FASTING PERSON KISSING OR EMBRACING

286. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that the Prophet would kiss while fasting.”888

287. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ziyād ibn ʿIlāqah narrated to us from ʿAmr ibn Maymūn from ʿĀ’ishah that the Prophet used to kiss889 while fasting.’”890

288. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘A man narrated to us from ʿĀmir ash-Shaʿbī from Masrūq that ʿĀ’ishah said that the Messenger of Allah used to kiss her face891 while fasting.’”892 893

Muḥammad said, “We see no harm in that if a man has control of himself894 so that it doesn’t lead to other things, i.e., ejaculation, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

289. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that the Prophet would be in contact, skin to skin, while fasting.”

Muḥammad said, “We see no harm in that as long as one does not fear for oneself that it will go further than being in contact, skin to skin,895 and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
