84. THE MERIT IN FASTING

293. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Saʿīd ibn Jubayr said, ‘Fasting the day of ʿĀshūrā’ is equal to fasting a year, and fasting the day of ʿArafah is equal to fasting two years, the year preceding it and the following year.’”908

294. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAlī ibn al-Aqmar narrated to us that the Prophet would fast continually909 and spend the night hungry910 standing in prayer. Then he would go to a portion911 of milk which had been put out for him and drink it,912 and it would be913 both his meal for breaking the fast and his pre-dawn meal until the same914 as it the next day.’ He said, ‘One time he went to get his drink and found that one of his Companions had fatigued himself915 and had drunk it.916 So they looked for food or drink for him917 in his wives’ chambers, but nothing was found. They asked his Companions but found nothing with any of them. He said, “Whoever feeds me, may Allah feed him,” twice, but they found nothing with which to feed him.’ He said, ‘So they turned to a she-goat and discovered that its udder was as full as it possibly918 could be with milk and so they milked some of it equal to the drink of the Messenger of Allah .’”919
