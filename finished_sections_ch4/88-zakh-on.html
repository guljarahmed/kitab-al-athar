88. ZAKĀH ON WORKING ANIMALS

307. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning pasturing944 horses kept for reproduction, ‘If you wish,945 there is a dinar on every horse, and if you wish ten dirhams, and if you wish, it is according to the value, so that on every two hundred dirhams there is five dirhams on every horse whether male or female.’”946

Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah used to adhere to all of this, but our verdict947 is that there is no zakāh on horses.”

308. It has reached us948 that the Prophet said, “I have annulled for my community’s sake the zakāh on horses949 and on slaves.”950 951

309. Muḥammad said, “Khaytham ibn ʿArāk ibn Mālik informed us saying, ‘I heard my father saying, “I heard Abū Hurayrah saying, ‘I heard the Messenger of Allah saying, “There is no zakāh to be paid by a Muslim man on his horse952 or his slave.”’”’”953 954

310. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is no zakāh on pasturing donkeys.’”955

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

311. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said, “There is no zakāh on bulls on which one works, nor is there zakāh on camels used for milling or for labouring.”’”956

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
