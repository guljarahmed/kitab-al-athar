124. SOMEONE WHO MARRIES WHILE ASSOCIATING PARTNERS WITH ALLAH AND THEN LATER ACCEPTS ISLAM

417. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a person who marries while associating partners with Allah and consummates the marriage with his wife, and then accepts Islam after that, and then later commits adultery, that he is not to be stoned until he is rendered muḥṣan1276 by marriage to a Muslim woman.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

418. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a couple are Jews or Christians and the husband accepts Islam, their marriage is still valid1277 whether the woman accepts Islam or not. If the wife becomes a Muslim [but not the husband], Islam is to be offered to the husband, and if he accepts Islam he may retain her within his original marriage, but if he refuses to accept Islam, they are separated. If the couple are Zoroastrians, and either one of them accepts Islam then the other is to be offered Islam, and if he or she accepts Islam, their original marriage is still valid, but if he or she refuses to accept Islam, then they are separated.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

419. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm was asked about a Jew and a Jewess who accept Islam, or a Christian man and woman, and he said, ‘They remain in their original marriage, and Islam only will increase them in good.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

420. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a man accepts Islam before consummating marriage with a woman who is a Zoroastrian, then Islam is offered to her. If she accepts, she is his wife. However, if she refuses to accept Islam they are separated, and she has no dowry1278 since the separation was because of her. If she accepts Islam before her husband and he had not yet consummated the marriage with her then Islam is offered to the husband. If he accepts Islam, she is his wife. However, if he refuses, they are separated and it is a final divorce1279 and she has a half of the dowry.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah. If the separation comes from the husband, it is a divorce and she has a half of the dowry since he was the one who refused Islam. However, if it is the woman who refused Islam, the separation comes from her and she has nothing of the dowry and her separation is not a divorce.”

421. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If the separation comes from the man it is divorce, but if it comes from the woman it is not divorce. If he had consummated the marriage with her, she has the full dowry. However, had he not consummated the marriage with her, she has no dowry, if the separation comes from her.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, except in one respect, because Abū Ḥanīfah1280 said, ‘If the husband reneges on Islam, the woman becomes finally separated from him but that is not a divorce,’1281 but in our verdict it is a divorce,1282 and that is the verdict of Ibrāhīm.”
