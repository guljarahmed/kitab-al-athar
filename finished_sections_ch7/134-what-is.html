134. WHAT IS ABHORENT OF SEXUAL INTERCOURSE WITH TWO SLAVE WOMEN WHO ARE SISTERS, AND OTHER THINGS

455. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a man has two sisters as slaves and has sexual intercourse with one of them, he may not have sexual intercourse with the other until someone else takes possession, through marriage or otherwise, of the right to sexual intercourse1343 with the one with whom he had sexual intercourse. If they are two sisters and one of them is his wife, but he has intercourse with the one who is a slave woman, then he should keep away from his wife until the slave woman has observed the ʿiddah due because of his semen.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this except in one respect: he ought not to have intercourse with his wife1344 if he has had sexual intercourse with her sister until someone else takes possession of the right to sexual intercourse with her sister whether through marriage or ownership, after she has become purified with a menstruation.1345 That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

456. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham from Ibn ʿUmar who said about two sisters who are slaves of a man with one of whom he has sexual intercourse, that he may not have intercourse with the other until someone else takes possession of the right to intercourse with the one with whom he has had intercourse.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

457. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he deplored1346 a man having intercourse with his slave woman along with her daughter, or his slave woman along with her sister or her paternal aunt or her maternal aunt. He used to deplore the same matters with respect to slave women which he deplored with respect to free women.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. Everything which is deplored in respect to marriage is deplored in respect to ownership except in one respect: one may collect as many slave women as one wishes,1347 but one may not marry more than four free women or four slave women.1348 That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
