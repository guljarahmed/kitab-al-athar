132. SOMEONE WHO MARRIES A WOMAN WHO HAS BEEN INFORMED OF THE DEATH OF HER HUSBAND

445. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb said concerning a man whose death is reported to his wife and so she remarries, and later the former husband returns, “The first is given the choice. If he wishes, his wife [is his], and if he wishes, then the dowry [is returned].”’ Abū Ḥanīfah said, ‘She is the former’s wife in every case.’”1328

Muḥammad said, “The like of that has reached us from ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib ,1329 and we adhere to it.”

446. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a woman who has lost her husband, ‘It has reached me what people say1330 [of her waiting for] four years, but that she wait is preferable to me.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

447. Similarly it has reached us from ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib who said about someone whose husband has gone missing, “She is a woman who has been tested, so let her be patient until [news of] his death comes to her or of his divorcing [her].”
