121. WHAT HAPPENS IF EACH OF TWO WOMEN IS MADE TO GO INTO [WHERE] THE OTHER WOMAN’S HUSBAND IS

411. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If each of two women is made to go into [where] the brother of her husband is, each of the two being made love to,1266 then each one of them is returned to her husband and she has a dowry inasmuch as the union was consummated, and her husband may not approach her until her ʿiddah expires.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Presumably this is by mistake, for otherwise it would seem that the punishment for transgressing the limit would be due. – Trans. ↩
