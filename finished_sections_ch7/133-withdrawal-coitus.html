133. WITHDRAWAL (COITUS INTERRUPTUS),1331 AND WHAT FORMS OF SEXUAL RELATIONS WITH WOMEN ARE FORBIDDEN

448. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Saʿīd ibn Jubayr who said, ‘Do not practice withdrawal1332 with a free woman without her consent, but as for a slave woman practise withdrawal without asking her consent.’”1333

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. However, if the slave woman is your wife, you may not practise withdrawal without her owner’s permission.1334 The slave woman’s permission is not to be sought in any of that, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

449. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd that he was asked about withdrawal and he said, ‘If Allah took the covenant from a soul in the spine of a man and yet he poured it out on a stone,1335 Allah would bring the living being out of it whose covenant He had taken. So practice withdrawal if you wish, and leave it if you wish.’”1336

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

450. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Abū Haytham al-Makkī narrated to us from Yūsuf ibn Māhik from Ḥafṣah the wife of the Prophet that a woman came to the Prophet and said that she had a husband who had sexual intercourse with her while she had her back turned to him and he said, “There is no harm in it if it is in a single orifice.”’”1337

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, and he only meant to say by his saying, ‘In a single orifice,’ if that was in the vagina, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

451. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Kathīr al-Aṣamm ar-Rammāḥ from Abū Zarrāʿ from Ibn ʿUmar that he [Abū Zarrāʿ] said, ‘I asked him about this āyah, “Your women are fertile fields for you, so come to your fertile fields however you like” (Sūrat al-Baqarah: 221). He said, “However you wish: practising withdrawal if you wish and not practising withdrawal if you wish.”’”1338

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

452. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥumayd al-Aʿraj narrated to us from a man from Abū Dharr who said, “The Messenger of Allah forbade having sexual intercourse with women in their posteriors.”’”1339 1340

453. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that the Prophet used to embrace1341 one of his wives while she was menstruating, and she was wearing an izār.”1342

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, and we see no harm in it, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

454. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘I play on the belly of my wife, while she is menstruating, until I have attained my desire.’”
