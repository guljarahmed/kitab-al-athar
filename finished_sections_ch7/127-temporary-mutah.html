127. TEMPORARY (MUTʿAH)1305 MARRIAGE

432. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said concerning temporary (mutʿah) marriage to women, ‘It was only granted as a concession to the Companions of Muḥammad when during some of their military expeditions they complained to him about their celibacy.1306 Subsequently it was abrogated by the āyah on marriage, inheritance and the dowry.’”1307

433. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Nāfiʿ narrated to us that Ibn ʿUmar said, “On the year of the military expedition against Khaybar, the Messenger of Allah forbade the meat of domestic donkeys and temporary marriages to women, and we were not fornicators.”’”1308

434. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ibn Shihāb az-Zuhrī from Muḥammad ibn ʿUbaydullāh from Saborah al-Juhanī from the Prophet that on the day of the Opening of Makkah [to Islam]1309 he forbade temporary marriage with women.”1310

435. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Yunus narrated the like of that to us from Rabīʿ ibn Sabirah al-Juhanī from his father from the Prophet concerning temporary marriage to women.’”1311 1312

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to all of this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
