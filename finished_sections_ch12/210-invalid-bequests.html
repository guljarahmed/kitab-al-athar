INHERITANCE AND BEQUESTS

210. INVALID BEQUESTS

652. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAṭā’ ibn as-Sā’ib narrated to us from his father from Saʿd ibn Abī Waqqāṣ who said, “The Prophet came to visit me when I was sick,” and he said, “I said, ‘Messenger of Allah, should I make a bequest of all of my wealth?’ and he said, ‘No.’ I said, ‘A half?’ He said, ‘No.’ I said, ‘A third1766?’ He said, ‘A third, and a third is a lot. Do not leave your family holding out their hands [to beg]1767 from people.’”’”1768

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. It is not valid to makes a bequest of more than a third to anyone. If someone makes a bequest of more than a third and the heirs1769 permit it after his death, it is permissible. An heir is not allowed to go back on anything he has permitted. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

653. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Qāsim ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān narrated to us from his father from ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd concerning a man who bequeaths a bequest which the heirs validate during his life1770 and then reject after his death, that he said, “That is denial; [the bequest] is not valid.”’”1771 1772

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The heirs’ validation of the bequest before his death is nothing.1773 If they permit it after his death and it is for one of the heirs or is more than a third,1774 it is valid, and they are unable to renege on that, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
