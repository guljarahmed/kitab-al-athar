227. A WIFE’S GIFT TO HER HUSBAND A HUSBAND’S TO HIS WIFE

708. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Husband and wife have the status of close relatives; whichever of them gives a gift to the other may not retract it.’”1861

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes
