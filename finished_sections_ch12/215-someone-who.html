215. SOMEONE WHO SETS A HALF OF HIS SLAVE FREE

674. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If a man in good health sets a half of his slave free then only that amount of him which he set free is free, and he must work to repay the rest of that which he has not set free.”

Muḥammad said, “That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him. As for our verdict it is that when he sets free any portion of him, whether great or small, then he has set all of him free,1801 and he is not set to work to repay anything at all. And Allah knows best.

Footnotes

  1. His relationship to a part of him is the same as his relationship to the whole, and so for this reason all of him is set free. ↩
