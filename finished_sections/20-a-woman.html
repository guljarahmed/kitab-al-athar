20. A WOMAN WHO EXPERIENCES227 IN DREAM228 WHAT A MAN EXPERIENCES

57. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm that Umm Sulaym bint Milḥān came to the Prophet to ask him about a woman experiencing in dream what men experience. The Prophet said, “If any of you women experience what men experience, let her take a ghusl.”’”229 230

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes
