12. WIPING THE FACE AFTER WUḌŪ’ WITH A HANDKERCHIEF AND TRIMMING THE MOUSTACHE

39. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who performs wuḍū’ and then wipes his face with a cloth, ‘There is no harm in it.’164 Then he said, ‘What do you think of the case in which he washes on a cold night: should he stand until he is dry?’”165 166

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to that, and we see no harm in it, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

40. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who cuts his nails or trims his hair [after having done wuḍū’], “Let him pass water over it.”’”167

Muḥammad said, “I heard Abū Ḥanīfah say, ‘Sometimes I cut my nails, trim my hair and do not pour water [over them] again until I pray.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of al-Ḥasan al-Baṣrī.”
