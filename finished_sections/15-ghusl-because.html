15. GHUSL BECAUSE OF SEXUAL RELATIONS (JANĀBAH)180

45. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn, said, “When the two circumcised parts181 meet, ghusl becomes obligatory.”’”182 183

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

46. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us, ‘Abū Isḥāq as-Sabīʿī narrated to us from al-Aswad ibn Yazīd that ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn, said, “The Messenger of Allah would make love184 to his wife at the beginning of the night and sleep without touching water.185 Then if he woke up in the latter part of the night he would do it again and take a ghusl.”’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. There is no harm186 when a man makes love to his wife if he sleeps before taking a ghusl or doing wuḍū’, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”

47. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAwn ibn ʿAbdullāh narrated to us from ash-Shaʿbī that ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib said, “It [the meeting of the two circumcised parts] makes the payment of the dowry obligatory.187 It invalidates divorce.188 It requires the [observance of the] ʿiddah period189 [after divorce or being widowed], and [yet] it doesn’t obligate190 [the use of] a ṣāʿ191 measure of water?”’”192 193

Muḥammad said, “When the two circumcised parts meet, then ghusl is obligatory whether or not one ejaculates, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”
