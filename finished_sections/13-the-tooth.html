13. THE TOOTH STICK

41. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Abū ʿAlī narrated to us from Tamām168 from Jaʿfar ibn Abī Ṭālib that the Prophet said, “How is it that I see you coming to me with yellow teeth?169 Use the tooth stick. If it were not that I would cause my community hardship, I would tell them to use the tooth stick for every prayer.”’”170

Muḥammad said, “The tooth stick is, in our view, a part of the Sunnah172 and ought not to be abandoned.”

42. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Both men and women in iḥrām173 can use the tooth stick.’”174

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”
