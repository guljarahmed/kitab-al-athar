6. PERFORMING WUḌŪ’ BECAUSE OF HAVING TOUCHED THE PENIS

22. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib said concerning touching the penis, ‘I do not care whether I have touched it or the end of my nose.’”101 102

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, to which we adhere.”103

23. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Ibn Masʿūd was asked about performing wuḍū’ because of having touched the penis and he said, ‘If it is so dirty, cut it off,’ meaning that there is no harm in it.”104 105

24. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Saʿd ibn Abī Waqqāṣ passed by a man washing his penis and asked, ‘What are you doing?106 Woe to you! This has not been made obligatory upon you.’”107

Muḥammad said, “And washing it is preferable to us108 if one urinates, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah.”
