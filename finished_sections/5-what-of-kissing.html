5. WHAT OF KISSING AND VOMITING BREAKS WUḌŪ'

20. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If you retch94 so much as to fill your mouth then repeat your wuḍū’, but if it is less than what fills your mouth do not repeat your wuḍū’.’”95

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, to which we adhere.”

21. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who returns from a journey96 and his maternal or paternal aunt or someone whom it would be forbidden97 for him to marry kisses him ‘He is not obliged to do wuḍū’ if someone whom he is forbidden to marry kisses him, but if someone whom it is permissible for him to marry kisses him then he is obliged to do wuḍū’, and it is of the same status as other matters which nullify wuḍū’ (ḥadath).”98

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Ibrāhīm, but we do not adhere to this, and we do not think that wuḍū’ must be done for kissing in any case except for when someone emits pre-ejaculatory fluid,100 for then it becomes obligatory because of the pre-ejaculatory fluid. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah .”
