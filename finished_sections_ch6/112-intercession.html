112. INTERCESSION

379. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he [Ḥammād] said, ‘I asked him about the statement of Allah, “It may be that those who are kāfir will wish that they had been Muslims.” (Sūrat al-Ḥijr: 2) He said, “Allah will punish some people of those who used to worship Him1144 and who did not worship other than Him, and some people who used to worship other than Him.1145 Then He will bring them together in the Fire, and the people who used to worship other than Allah will reproach those who used to worship Him and will say, ‘He is punishing us because We worshipped other than Him, but your worship of Him has not helped you for you are being punished along with us.’ Then the Lord will grant permission to the angels and the prophets and they will intercede, so that there will remain none in the Fire who used to worship Him but He will bring him out, so much so that Iblīs will look up [in hope] towards the intercession because of his former worship.” He said,”1146 So He says, ‘It may be that those who are kāfir1147 will wish that they had been Muslims.’”’”

380. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ribʿī ibn Ḥarrāsh al-ʿAbsī that Ḥudhayfah ibn al-Yamān said, ‘Some putrid1148 people will enter the Garden whom the Fire has completely burnt.’”1149

381. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Salamah ibn Kuhayl from Abū’z-Zaʿrā’1150 that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd 1151 said, ‘Allah will punish some people from among the people of īmān for their wrong actions, and then He will bring them out by the intercession of Muḥammad until there only remain in the Fire those Allah mentioned, “‘What caused you to enter Saqar?1152 They will say, ‘We were not among those who did ṣalāh1153 and we did not feed the poor. We plunged1154 with those who plunged and denied the Day of Judgment until the Certain1155 came to us.’ The intercession of the interceders will not help them.”1156 (Sūrat al-Muddaththir: 41-47)’”

382. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from ʿAṭiyyah al-ʿAwfī that Abū Saʿīd al-Khudrī said, ‘The Messenger of Allah said, “Let whoever deliberately attributes a lie to me prepare his seat in the Fire.”’1157 He said, ‘I asked him about this āyah, “And stay awake for prayer during part of the night1158 as a supererogatory action for yourself.1159 It may well be that your Lord will raise you to a Praiseworthy Station.” (Sūrat al-Isrā’: 79) He said, “The Praiseworthy Station is intercession.”’1160 He said, ‘Allah will punish some of the people of īmān for their wrong actions and will then bring them out [of the Fire] by the intercession of Muḥammad , and they will be brought to a river1161 called Life1162 in which they will bathe1163 like the bathing of small cucumbers.1164 Then they will enter the Garden and they will be called the Jahannamis, but then they will petition Allah1165 and He will remove that name from them.’”1166 1167

383. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us of the like of this from Shaddād ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān from Abū Saʿīd al-Khudrī .”

384. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Yazīd ibn Ṣuhayb (the one known as “The Faqīr1168 [Needy]”) from Jābir ibn ʿAbdullāh al-Anṣārī who [Yazīd] said, ‘I asked him [Jābir] about intercession and he said, “Allah will punish some of the people of īmān1169 and then bring them out [of the Fire] by the intercession of Muḥammad .”’ He said, ‘I said to him, “So what about the statement of Allah, ‘They will want to get out of the Fire but they will not be able to.1170 They will have an everlasting punishment’? (Sūrat al-Mā’idah: 37).” He said, “This is about those who are kāfir; read what precedes it.”’”1171
