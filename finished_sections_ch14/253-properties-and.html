253. PROPERTIES AND PRE-EMPTION1975

764. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from Shurayḥ who said, ‘Pre-emption [belongs to those dwelling] in front of the doors.’”1976

“We do not adhere to this. Pre-emption belongs to adjoining1977 neighbours, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

765. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is only pre-emption in the case of land or a dwelling.’”

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

766. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAbd al-Karīm narrated to us from al-Miswar ibn Makhramah from Rāfiʿ1978 ibn Khadīj who said, “Saʿd offered me a house of his and he said, ‘Take it, for I was given more1979 for it than you give me for it, but you have more right because I heard the Messenger of Allah saying, “The neighbour has more right to pre-emption (saqb).”’”’”1980 1981

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
