245. ADVANCE PAYMENT FOR FABRIC

748. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If someone pays in advance for fabric and its width and [substance or] thickness are known, it is valid, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”1943

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this if its length, width, thickness, its type and the period of time are all named and the price is paid in ready money before they separate, then it is valid.”

749. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who pays with fabric in advance for fabric, ‘If they are of different types, there is no harm in it.’”1944

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
