252. LOANS

761. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who lent another man silver, and that man brought him back more than it, ‘Silver for silver, and I abhor excess in it.1970 Let him bring the same as it.’”1971

“We do not adhere to this. There is no harm in this as long as it is not a precondition he laid on him. If it is a precondition he laid on him, there is no good in it. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

762. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm concerning a man who lends another man dirhams on the condition that he pay him back in Rayy.1972 He said, ‘I abhor it.’”1973

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

763. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Every loan which draws some benefit1974 [to the person making the loan] has no good in it.’”

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

  3. He repays the loan and as an extra transports the coins to Rayy. ↩
  4. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.184. ↩
