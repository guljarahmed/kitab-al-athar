247. CONVEYING TRADE TO LAND AT WAR (DĀR AL-ḤARB)

751. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a trader who goes regularly to land at war, ‘There is no harm in that as long as he does not convey weapons, horses (kurāʿ)1951 or booty (salab).”1952 1953

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. kurāʿ literally means that which is below the ankle in animals. Then horses in particular were given this as a name. [Imam] Muḥammad said that kurāʿ are horses, mules and donkeys. ↩
  2. Presumably a prohibition of his taking booty seized from the enemy back to them to sell it to them. – Trans. ↩
  3. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.195. ↩
