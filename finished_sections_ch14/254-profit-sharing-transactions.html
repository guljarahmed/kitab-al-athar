254. PROFIT-SHARING TRANSACTIONS1982 FOR A THIRD, AND PROFIT-SHARING TRANSACTIONS WITH THE PROPERTY OF ORPHANS, AND BECOMING PARTNERS WITH THEM

767. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who gives property in a profit-sharing transaction for a third [of the profits] or a half, with an added ten dirhams,1983 ‘There is no good1984 in this. What would you think if he did not gain a dirham in profit, what would he have?’”1985

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

768. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah said, ‘If I were put in charge1986 of the property of an orphan, I would intermix his food with my food and his drink with my drink and would not consider it the same status as an impurity.’”1987

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

769. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning the property of orphans, ‘The person entrusted [with responsibility for the orphan] may do whatever he wishes to do: if he thinks he should deposit it in a safe place, he may do so, and if he thinks he should trade with it, he may trade with it, and if he thinks he should pay it over as a profit-sharing loan, he may do so.’”

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

770. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Saʿīd ibn Jubayr spoke concerning this āyah, ‘Those who are wealthy1988 should abstain from it altogether. Those who are poor should use it sensibly and correctly1989’ (Sūrat an-Nisā’: 6) and he said, ‘[...should use it sensibly and correctly] as a loan1990 [which they repay later].’”

771. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham from a man that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, ‘The person entrusted [with responsibility for the orphan] should not consume1991 any of the orphan’s property, neither as a loan [to be repaid later] nor in any other way.’”

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

772. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Layth ibn Abī Sulaym narrated to us from Mujāhid that Ibn Masʿūd said, “There is no zakāh on the property of an orphan.”’”1992

“We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Then Allah revealed, “They will ask you about the property of orphans. Say, ‘Managing it in their best interests is best.’ If you mix your property with theirs, they are your brothers” (Sūrat an-Nisā’: 218). ↩
