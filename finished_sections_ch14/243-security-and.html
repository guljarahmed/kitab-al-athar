243. SECURITY AND A PAWNED ITEM1935 MADE IN ADVANCE PAYMENT

745. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm who said, “There is no harm1936 in a pawned item or something left as security in advance payment.”’”1937

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

746. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning an advance payment made in fulūs (small change) for which one takes a security, ‘There is no harm in it.’”1938

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
