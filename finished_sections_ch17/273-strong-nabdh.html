273. STRONG NABĪDH

832. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād who said, ‘I used to be wary of nabīdh, and then I went to see Ibrāhīm while he was eating and ate with him. He was brought a vessel of nabīdh, but when he saw my hesitation2092 over it he said, “ʿAlqamah narrated to me from ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd that he [ʿAlqamah] would sometimes eat with him and then he [ʿAbdullāh] would call for some of his nabīdh2093 which Sīrīn, ʿAbdullāh’s umm walad, had prepared for him and he drank and gave me to drink.”’”2094

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

833. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Mazāḥim ibn Zufar narrated to us that aḍ-Ḍaḥḥāk ibn Mazāhim said, “Abū ʿUbaydah2095 went off and then he showed him glazed clay vessels of ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd in which nabīdh2096 was prepared for him.’”2097

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

834. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Abū Isḥāq as-Sabīʿī narrated to us from ʿAmr ibn Maymūn al-Awdī that ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb said, “The Muslims have camels2098 [which they slaughter] for their food, and the old camels among them belong to the family of ʿUmar, and nothing digests (lit.: cuts) the meat of these camels in their bellies except for strong nabīdh.’”2099

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

835. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said that ʿUmar was brought a desert Arab who had become drunk. He tried to look for an excuse for him,2100 but when he couldn’t do that (because of the departure of his intellect) he said, ‘Lock him up and when he is sober, flog him.’ He called for the remains left in the small waterskin and tasted it, discovering it to be impossibly strong nabīdh and so he called for water and diluted it (and ʿUmar used to like strong drink). Then he drank and gave the people sitting with him to drink. He said, ‘Dilute this with water when its shayṭān overcomes you.’”2101 2102

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
