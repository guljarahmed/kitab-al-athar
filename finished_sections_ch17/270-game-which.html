270. GAME WHICH ONE SHOOTS

821. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who shoots game or hits it, ‘If he cuts it in two halves, eat both of them. If that which is closer to the head is smaller, eat both of them. If that which is closer to the head is larger, eat that which is closer to the head and throw away what remains of that which is closer to the rear.2072 If you cut a piece off it or a limb and it is clearly separate, do not eat it unless it is attached. If it is attached, eat it.’”2073

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

822. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Saʿīd ibn Jubayr from Ibn ʿAbbās and he [Saʿīd ibn Jubayr] said, ‘A black slave came to him [Ibn ʿAbbās] and said, “I am with the domestic cattle belonging to my family and I am on the way: may I give others some of their milk to drink?”2074 He said, “No.” He said, “I shoot game and it dies in front of me2075 or goes out of my sight and later dies2076?” He said, “Eat what you shoot which dies, and leave that which goes out of your sight and later dies.”’”2077 2078

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him. He only meant by his words ‘you shoot it and it dies’ as long as it does not hide itself from your sight. ‘That which goes out of your sight and later dies,’ is that which hides itself from your sight. If it hides itself from your sight and you search for it until you come upon it, and it has no wounds on it apart from your arrow, there is no harm in eating it.”

823. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If you shoot game and say the Name [of Allah],2079 if you have cut it in two halves [with the spear or arrow], eat it. If that portion which is adjoining the head is larger, eat that which adjoins the head but don’t eat anything else. If you have severed a paw from it or a foot or any other part, eat all of it except for the part you have severed.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
