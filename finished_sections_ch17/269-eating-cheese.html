269. EATING CHEESE

820. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAṭiyyah al-ʿAwfī narrated to us from Ibn ʿUmar and he [ʿAṭiyyah] said, “I was sitting with him [Ibn ʿUmar] when a man came to him and asked him about cheese. He said, ‘What is cheese?’ He said, ‘Something manufactured from the milk of goats, and in general it is the Zoroastrians who manufacture it.’ He said, ‘Mention the name of Allah and eat it.’”’”2071

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
