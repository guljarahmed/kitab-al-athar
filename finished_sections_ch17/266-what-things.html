FOOD AND DRINK

266. WHAT THINGS ARE ABHORED OF SHEEP AND GOATS, AND BLOOD AND OTHER THINGS

811. Muḥammad said, “ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān ibn ʿAmr al-Awzāʿī informed us from Wāṣil ibn Abī Jamīl that Mujāhid said, ‘The Messenger of Allah abhored seven things from the sheep: the gall-bladder, the urinary bladder, the glands, the vulva, the penis, the testicles and the blood. The Messenger of Allah used to like the forepart of sheep and goats.’”
