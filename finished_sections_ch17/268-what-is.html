268. WHAT IS ABHORED OF EATING THE MEAT OF PREDATORS AND THE MILK OF DONKEYS

816. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah was given a gift of a large lizard2060 and so asked the Prophet about eating it and he forbade her.2061 A beggar came and she wanted to give it to him to eat, and he said, ‘Would you feed him with that which you would not eat?’”2062

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

817. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Makḥūl ash-Shāmī narrated to us from the Prophet that he forbade [eating] any predator2063 that possesses fangs and any bird that possesses claws,2064 [and he forbade] sexual intercourse with pregnant women2065 taken in battle, and eating the meat of domestic donkeys.’”2066

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

818. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham that Ibn ʿAbbās disapproved2067 of horseflesh.”2068

Muḥammad said, “This is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, but we do not adhere to it, and we see no harm in horseflesh. Many traditions2069 are narrated on its permissibility.”

819. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There is no good in the meat of donkeys and their milk.’”2070

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
