271. GAME CAUGHT BY A DOG

824. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿAdī ibn Ḥātim asked the Messenger of Allah about game which a dog kills before one reaches it to slaughter it, and the Prophet told him to eat it if [the dog] is trained.”2080 2081

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

825. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If your trained dog catches for you [and kills its prey] then eat. If an untrained dog catches for you [and kills its prey], do not eat it.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

826. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from Saʿīd ibn Jubayr that Ibn ʿAbbās said, ‘That which your dog catches for you [and kills], if it is trained, eat it. If it has eaten [of it], do not eat because it has only caught it for itself. As for the hawk and the falcon, eat even if it has [already] eaten, because the extent that indicates that it is trained2082 is that if you call it, it will come to you, and it is unable to withstand being beaten2083 to give up the food.’”2084

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

827. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning someone who sends out his dog but forgets to mention the Name and it catches and kills [the game], ‘I abhor eating it, and if a Jew or a Christian2085 [dispatched the dog] then it is the same as that.’”

Muḥammad said, “We do not adhere to this. There is no harm in eating it if he left out the Name from forgetfulness, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

828. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Qatādah narrated to us from Abū Qalābah from Abū Thaʿlabah al-Khushanī from the Prophet and [Abū Thaʿlabah] said, “We said, ‘We go to the land of those who associate partners with Allah (mushrikūn); may we eat from their vessels?’ He said, ‘If there is no avoiding it, wash them and then eat from them.’ We said, ‘We are in a land [where we hunt] game?’ He said, ‘Eat that which your arrow gains for you or your horse or your dog, if it is trained,’ and he forbade us to eat any predator with fangs, any bird with claws, or to eat the meat of the domestic donkey.”’”2086

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
