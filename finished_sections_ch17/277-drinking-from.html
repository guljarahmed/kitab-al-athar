277. DRINKING FROM GOLD AND SILVER VESSELS

845. Muḥammad said, “Abū Farwah2125 narrated to us from ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān ibn Abī Laylā from Ḥudhayfah ibn al-Yamān and he [ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān] said, ‘I was a guest of Dihqān2126 in Madā’in2127 along with Ḥudhayfah , and he [Dihqān] brought us food and we ate. Ḥudhayfah called for some drink and he brought us something to drink in a silver vessel, and so he took the vessel and struck his (Dihqān’s) face with it. What he did upset us.’ He said, ‘So he said,2128 “Do you realise why I have done this?” I said, “No.” He said, “I alighted with him as his guest once last year and he brought me a drink in it2129 and I informed him that the Messenger of Allah forbade us2130 to eat in vessels of gold and silver or to drink from them, and not to wear silk and brocade, because both are for idolaters in this world and for us in the next life.”’”2131

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
