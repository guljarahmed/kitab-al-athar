LEGAL JUDGEMENTS

261. SOMEONE WHO MAKES A CLAIM OF A RIGHT FROM ANOTHER MAN

786. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Clear evidence is required of a claimant, and to swear an oath is the right of the person against whom the claim is made,’2020 and he would never return the [right to swear an] oath [to a claimant].”2021 2022

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
