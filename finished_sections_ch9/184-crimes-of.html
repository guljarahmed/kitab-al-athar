184. CRIMES OF SLAVES FOR WHOM HAS BEEN WRITTEN A CONTRACT FOR THEM TO PURCHASE THEIR FREEDOM, SLAVES TO BE SET FREE ON THE DEATHS OF THEIR OWNERS, AND SLAVE WOMEN WHO ARE MOTHERS OF HEIRS (UMM WALAD)

584. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that, ‘Crimes of slaves for whom there has been written a contract for them to purchase their freedom (mukātab), slaves who are to be set free on the deaths of their owners (mudabbar), and slave women who are mothers of heirs (umm walad) are the responsibility of their owner [to pay compensatory payments].’”1639 1640

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this except that we think the crime of a mukātab slave is his responsibility within his price, whichever is the lesser: the compensatory payment or his price. As for the mudabbar slave and the umm walad, the owner is responsible for whichever is the lesser: the compensatory payment for the injury or her price, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

585. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning an umm walad slave woman and a mudabbarah slave woman who cause injuries, ‘Their owner is responsible for the injuries they cause because the act of setting them free has already taken effect,1641 and so he is unable to use them in payment [of the compensatory payment] nor do the ʿāqilah pay, because they are both still slaves.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

586. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from Shurayḥ who said, ‘With respect to ḥadd punishments and testimony, a mukātab slave is still a slave as long as he owes a single dirham.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
