181. PEOPLE WHO DIG [UNDER] A WALL, WHICH FALLS ON THEM

578. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he spoke concerning people who dig [under] a wall and the wall falls on them. He said, ‘They must pay the compensatory payment to each other.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this except that one removes from each person’s compensatory payment his own portion, so that if they are four persons, a quarter of the compensatory payment is annulled for each one of them, and if they are three, a third of the compensatory payment is annulled for each one of them. That is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
