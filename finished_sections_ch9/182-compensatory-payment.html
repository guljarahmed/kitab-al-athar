182. COMPENSATORY PAYMENT FOR WOMEN AND THEIR INJURIES

579. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us from Ibrāhīm who said, “The verdict of ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib is preferable to me than the verdict of ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd, Zayd ibn Thābit and Shurayḥ concerning injuries to women1626 and men.”’”1627

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to the verdict of ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib and Ibrāhīm. ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib used to say, ‘Injuries to women are evaluated as being worth a half of men’s injuries in every case.’ ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd and Shurayḥ used to say, ‘They are equal with respect to teeth and wounds which lay bare the bone and then they are a half in everything else.’ Zayd ibn Thābit used to say,1628 ‘They are equal up until a third of the full compensatory payment,1629 and then they are a half in everything else.’ The verdict of ʿAlī ibn Abī Ṭālib concerning the half is preferable to us in every case, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

580. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘For [damage to or destruction of] the nipple of a woman’s breast a half of the full compensatory payment must be paid, and for two nipples the full compensatory payment must be paid.’”1630 1631

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. For a man’s two nipples, the compensation must be assessed by a just and knowledgeable person, and this is all the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
