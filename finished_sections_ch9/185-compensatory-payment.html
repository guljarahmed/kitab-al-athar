185. COMPENSATORY PAYMENT FOR A NON-MUSLIM LIVING UNDER MUSLIM RULE

587. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham ibn Abī’l-Haytham that the Prophet , Abū Bakr, ʿUmar and ʿUthmān said, ‘Compensatory payment for a non-Muslim living under Muslim rule is the same as the compensatory payment for a free Muslim.’”1642 1643

588. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād informed us that Ibrāhīm said, “Compensatory payment for a non-Muslim living under Muslim rule is the same as the compensatory payment for a free Muslim.”’”

589. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Abū’l-ʿAṭūf from az-Zuhrī that Abū Bakr, ʿUmar and ʿUthmān regarded compensatory payment for a Christian and compensatory payment for a Jew as the same as compensatory payment for a free Muslim.”1644

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, and it is the same with the Zoroastrian in our view, and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

590. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that a man of [the tribe of] Bakr ibn Wā’il killed a man of the people of Ḥīrah.1645 ʿUmar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb wrote concerning it, ‘Give him over to the heirs of the deceased, and they can kill him if they wish and they may pardon him if they wish.’ So the man was given over to the heir of the deceased and [then] on to a man said to have been Khuthayn, one of the people of Ḥīrah, who killed him. After that ʿUmar wrote concerning it, ‘If the man has not already been killed, do not kill him,’ and they thought that ʿUmar had wanted to satisfy them with the compensatory payment.”1646

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. If a Muslim deliberately kills a non-Muslim living under Muslim governance then he may be killed for it, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him. Similarly, it has reached us from the Prophet1647 that he killed a Muslim because of a non-Muslim living under Muslim governance [whom the Muslim had killed] and said, ‘I am the truest of those who fulfil their covenants.’”
