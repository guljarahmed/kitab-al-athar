298. DYEING WITH HENNA AND INDIGO

901. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿUthmān ibn ʿAbdullāh narrated to us, “Umm Salamah, the wife of the Prophet , brought us some combings2216 of the hair of the Messenger of Allah dyed with henna.”’”2217

902. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād who said, ‘I asked Ibrāhīm about dyeing with indigo2218 and he said, “A pleasant herb,” and saw no harm in that.’”2219

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

903. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Abū Ḥujayyah narrated to us from Ibn Buraydah2220 from Abū’l-Aswad ad-Du’alī from Abū Dharr that the Prophet said, “The best of things with which you can alter hair are henna and indigo.”’”2221 2222

904. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Muḥammad ibn Qays narrated to us, “The head of al-Ḥusayn ibn ʿAlī was brought and I gazed at his beard and head which seemed to have been dyed2223 with indigo.”’”2224

905. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Yazīd ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān from Anas ibn Mālik , ‘It is as if I were gazing at the beard of Abū Quḥāfah2225 which was like a blaze2226 of ʿarfaj,’2227 meaning because of the intensity of its redness, and Allah knows best.”2228

What is meant by the hadith is either to dye with both henna and indigo together or with each of them individually. It would seem more likely that what is meant is to use indigo separately from henna because if henna is used along with indigo it produces black and the prohibition of black is ṣaḥīḥ, and so it is more probable that the hadith about henna or indigo is a choice. Nevertheless the differing hadith narrations that refer to henna and indigo are reckoned as referring to using henna one time so that the colour is red and using indigo sometimes so that the colour is green, but if they are used together the colour becomes red inclining to black, and thus what would be meant would be to use a mix of henna and indigo (Mirqāt). ↩
