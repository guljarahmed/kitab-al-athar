297. PLUCKING OUT (ḤAFF) THE HAIR OF THE FACE

It is said that a woman ḥaffat her face i.e., she removed hair from it

897. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm from ʿĀ’ishah the Mother of the Mu’minūn that a woman asked her, ‘Shall I pluck the hair on my face?’ And she said, ‘Remove harm2213 from yourself.’”2214

898. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ziyād ibn ʿIlāqah narrated to us from ʿAmr ibn Maymūn from ʿĀ’ishah that a woman asked her, ‘Shall I pluck out the hair on my face?’ And she said, ‘Remove harm from yourself.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

899. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that he used to abhor marking an animal on its face or striking it on the face.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”

900. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham that Ibn ʿUmar used to grasp his beard with his hand and cut off what was below the grasp of his hand.”2215

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
