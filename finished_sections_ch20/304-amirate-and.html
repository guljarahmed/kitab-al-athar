304. AMIRATE, AND “WHOEVER ESTABLISHES A SUNNAH WHICH THOSE AFTER HIM ACTED UPON”

914. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘There are three things for which the dead are rewarded after their death: a son who supplicates for the deceased [father] after his death and [the father] is rewarded by his supplication, and a man who learnt knowledge which he acts upon and which he teaches people, and he is rewarded for that which he acted upon or taught, and a man who left land as ṣadaqah.’”

915. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Abū Ghassān2245 from al-Ḥasan al-Baṣrī that the Prophet said, ‘Abū Dharr! amirate is a trust, but on the Day of Rising it will be a humiliation and a regret except for the one who takes charge of it according to its right and discharges the duty incumbent on him with respect to it, and how can he possibly do that,2246 Abū Dharr?’”2247

916. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Trial is entrusted with words.’”2248 2249
