286. ONE WHO SHOWS THE WAY TO GOOD ACTION IS THE SAME AS SOMEONE WHO DOES IT

877. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAlqamah ibn Marthad, who ascribed the hadith with a chain of transmission (marfūʿ) to the Messenger of Allah , said, “A man came asking him to give him a mount as a conveyance [in the way of Allah] and the Messenger of Allah said, ‘I don’t have anything on which to mount you, but I will point out one of the youth of the Anṣār for you. Go to him, and you will find him in the graveyard of Banī so-and-so practising archery with some of his companions, because he has a camel on which he will mount you.’ The man went to the graveyard of Banī so-and-so and found him there practising archery with some of his companions. He said to him, ‘I went to the Messenger of Allah asking him to give me a mount as a conveyance [in the way of Allah], but I didn’t find anything with him,’ and so he told the tale. He [the young man] said, ‘By Allah the One Whom there is no god but Him! Did the Messenger of Allah mention2181 this to you?’ He said that to him twice. Then he went and mounted him. He came back to the Prophet on the camel and told the Prophet the story. The Prophet said to him, ‘Go, for the person who shows the way to a good action is the same as the one who does it.’”’”2182
