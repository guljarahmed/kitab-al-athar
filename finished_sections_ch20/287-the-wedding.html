287. THE WEDDING FEAST

878. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from al-Haytham who said, ‘When the Prophet married Umm Salamah he gave a gruel of parched barley (sawīq) and dates as a marriage feast for her, and said, ‘I will spend seven [nights] with you and seven2183 with your co-wives, if you wish.’” Muḥammad said, “Meaning that he would stay with her for seven [nights] and with [each of] her co-wives for seven.”2184

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Meaning, “I will treat you and them equally and appoint for each one of them seven [days and nights] just as I spent seven with you.” ↩
  2. Ibn Saʿd narrated the second half of it by different routes; see what is in aṭ-Ṭabaqāt al-kubrā, vol.8, pp.92-4, and Muslim narrated it under “breast-feeding” in the chapter on “the respect which old age is due.” ↩
