291. GENTLE CONDUCT AND MEANNESS

887. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ayyūb ibn ʿĀ’idh narrated to us from Mujāhid who ascribed it directly to the Prophet as a marfūʿ hadith that he said, “If people were to look at the quality of gentleness, they would not see any more beautiful creation of Allah. If they were to look at evil disposition, they would not see any uglier creation of Allah.”’”
