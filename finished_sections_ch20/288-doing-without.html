288. DOING WITHOUT (ZUHD)

879. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us that Ibrāhīm said, “The family of Muḥammad did not eat their fill2185 from wheaten bread on three consecutive days until Muḥammad left the world. The world was continuously difficult and troublesome until Muḥammad was taken. When he was taken, the world turned to them, pouring forth.”’”2186

Footnotes

  1. This was by his choice . ↩
  2. Al-Bukhārī narrated the like of it under “foods” in the chapter on “that which the Prophet and his Companions used to eat,” and Muslim under “doing-without,” in both of which there is “two consecutive days.” ↩
