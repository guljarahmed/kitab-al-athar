300. RECORDING KNOWLEDGE

909. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm used to abhor writing but later he regarded it as good.2234 Ḥammād said, ‘And I saw Ibrāhīm writing down after that.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. At first the Prophet prohibited writing for fear of the hadith resembling the Qur’ān and then later when the Qur’ān was firmly established in that it was memorised he permitted them to write hadith. ↩
