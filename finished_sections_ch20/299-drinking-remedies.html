299. DRINKING REMEDIES, COW’S MILK AND CAUTERISATION

906. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Qays ibn Muslim narrated to us from Ṭāriq ibn Shihāb that ʿAbdullāh ibn Masʿūd said, “Allah has not placed an illness without placing a remedy for it, except for death2229 and senility,2230 so you must use cow’s milk because it is blended from every shrub.”’”2231

907. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ narrated to us that Abū Hurayrah said, “The Messenger of Allah said, ‘When the Pleiades2232 rise the pests of the people of every land are removed.’”’”2233

908. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that Khabbāb ibn al-Aratt cauterised his son ʿAbdullāh because of an ulcer [in the neck].”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
