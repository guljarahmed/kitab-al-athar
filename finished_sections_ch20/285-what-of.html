285. WHAT OF YOUR CHILDREN’S PROPERTY IS PERMISSIBLE FOR YOU

875. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād from Ibrāhīm that ʿĀ’ishah said, ‘The best thing which you can eat is your own earnings, and [the earnings of] your children are a part of your earnings.’”2179

Muḥammad said, “There is no harm, if one is in need, in eating from one’s son’s earnings in a moderate fashion. If one is of independent means2180 and takes something from him, it is a debt which one owes, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
