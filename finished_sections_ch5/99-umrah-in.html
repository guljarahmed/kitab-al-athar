99. ʿUMRAH IN THE MONTHS OF THE HAJJ AND AT OTHER TIMES

338. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning the man who sets out on the ʿUmrah outside of the months of the Hajj and then takes up residence until he does the Hajj or returns1033 to his family and then performs the Ḥajj,1034 that he is not performing tamattuʿ.1035 If he sets out on ʿUmrah during the months of the Hajj and then returns to his family and then does the Hajj, he is not performing tamattuʿ. If he performs ʿUmrah in the months of Hajj and then takes up residence1036 until he performs the Hajj, he is performing tamattuʿ.”1037

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

339. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man of the people of Makkah1038 who performed ʿUmrah in the months of Hajj and then later performed Hajj in that1039 same year, ‘He does not have to sacrifice a hady offering for his tamattuʿ.’”1040

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him. That is because of the words of Allah, exalted is He, ‘That is for anyone whose family does not live near the Masjid al-Ḥarām.’” (Sūrat al-Baqarah: 195)”

340. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who comes as someone performing tamattuʿ1041 in the month of Ramaḍān but does not perform ṭawāf until he enters Shawwāl, ‘He is performing tamattuʿ because he performed ṭawāf in the months of Hajj.’”1042

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. His ʿUmrah is in the month in which he performs the ṭawāf and not in the month in which he enters iḥrām, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

341. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning a man who misses1043 fasting three days during the Hajj, ‘He must sacrifice a hady offering.1044 There is no exemption from it even if he has to sell his clothes.’”1045

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

342. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Yazīd ibn ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān narrated to us from an old woman1046 of al-ʿAtīk that ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn, said, “There is no harm in ʿUmrah in any part of the year you wish, except for five days:1047 the Day of ʿArafah, the Day of Sacrifice, and the Days of Drying of the Meat (tashrīq).”’”1048

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, except that we say, ‘The afternoon1049 of ʿArafah’; as for the morning1050 of ʿArafah there is no harm in ʿUmrah then.”
