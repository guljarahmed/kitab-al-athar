102. WHOEVER SACRIFICES HAS BECOME FREE OF IḤRĀM

350. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād concerning tamattuʿ, ‘Whoever sacrifices the hady offering on the Day of Sacrifice has left iḥrām.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this when he shaves1065 [his head or trims his hair] except that women in particular do not become permissible to him [in intercourse] until he visits the House and performs the ṭawāf of Visiting. As for things other than women and perfume,1066 that is permissible to him if he shaves his head before performing ṭawāf of the House, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Because shaving is one of the causes of leaving iḥrām as is sacrifice. ↩
  2. Perhaps “perfume” is extra. ↩
