104. WHOEVER BECOMES IN NEED BECAUSE OF SOME ILLNESS WHILE IN IḤRĀM

353. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning cracking of the skin1069 when a woman is in iḥrām, ‘Oil it with clarified butter and fat.’ Saʿīd ibn Jubayr said, ‘With anything that you eat.’”1070

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to the verdict of Saʿīd as long as there is no scent in it, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

354. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Ḥammād narrated to us saying, “I asked Ibrāhīm, ‘Can the person in iḥrām bathe?’ He answered, ‘Allah will not do anything with his dirt.’”’”1071

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and see no harm in it, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

355. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning the nail of a person in iḥrām which breaks, ‘He should break it.’ Saʿīd ibn Jubayr said, ‘He should cut it off.’”

Muḥammad said, “All of that is good, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

356. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Both men and women in iḥrām use the tooth stick.’”1072

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”
