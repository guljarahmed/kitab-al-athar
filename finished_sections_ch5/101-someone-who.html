101. SOMEONE WHO HAS SEXUAL INTERCOURSE WITH HIS WIFE WHILE IN IḤRĀM

346. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from ʿAbd al-ʿAzīz ibn Rāfiʿ from Mujāhid from Ibn ʿAbbās that a man came to him and said, ‘I kissed my wife while in iḥrām, but I limited my appetite to that,’ so he said, ‘You are affected by violent sexual desire. You must spill blood [by sacrificing]1060 and your Hajj is complete.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this. The Hajj isn’t invalidated until the two circumcised parts meet, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, and the like has reached us from ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ.”

347. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ that Ibn ʿAbbās said, ‘If someone has sexual intercourse after pressing on1061 from ʿArafah, he must sacrifice a she-camel,1062 perform what remains of his Hajj and his Hajj is complete.’”1063

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

348. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Saʿīd ibn Jubayr that Ibn ʿUmar said, ‘If he has sexual intercourse after pressing on from ʿArafāt he must spill blood [in sacrifice], perform what remains of his Hajj, and the subsequent Hajj is obligatory on him.’”

Muḥammad said, “We do not adhere to this verdict; the right verdict is that which Ibn ʿAbbās gave.”

349. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘Whoever kisses while in iḥrām must spill blood [in sacrifice].’”1064

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this, if he kisses with sexual passion, and that is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes
