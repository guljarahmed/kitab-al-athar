100. PRAYER AT ʿARAFAH AND MUZDALIFAH (JAMʿ)

343. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘If you pray on the day of ʿArafah in your shelter1051 then pray each one of the two prayers1052 at their time, and do not depart from your lodging until you have prayed the prayer.’”1053 1054

Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, used to adhere to this,1055 but our verdict is that he should pray them in his shelter as he prays them with the imam, joining them together with one adhān and two iqāmahs, because ʿAṣr is only brought forward to facilitate the standing.1056 Thus it has reached us from ʿĀ’ishah, the Mother of the Mu’minūn, and from ʿAbdullāh ibn ʿUmar and from ʿAṭā’ ibn Abī Rabāḥ and Mujāhid (may Allah have mercy on them both).”

344. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning prayer at Muzdalifah (Jamʿ), ‘When you pray them1057 at Muzdalifah, pray them with a single iqāmah,1058 but if you pray voluntary prayers in between them, say the iqāmah for each one.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, but it does not please us1059 that someone do voluntary prayers in between them.”

345. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm did not use to leave his home [in Kufa] on the Day of ʿArafah. Abū Ḥanīfah said, ‘This going forth to the desert and praying there [taʿrīf] which people do on the Day of ʿArafah is an innovation. Going forth to the desert and praying there [taʿrīf] is only to be done at ʿArafāt.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”
