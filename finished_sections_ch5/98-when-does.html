98. WHEN DOES ONE CEASE THE LABBAYK? AND MAKING STIPULATIONS CONCERNING THE HAJJ

336. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said, ‘The person in iḥrām ceases saying “At Your service...” for the ʿUmrah when he salutes1031 the Stone, and he ceases saying, “At Your service...” for the Hajj at the first pebble with which he pelts the pillar at al-ʿAqabah.’”1032

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

337. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us from Ḥammād that Ibrāhīm said concerning the man who stipulates concerning the Hajj, ‘His stipulation is nothing.’”

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him.”

Footnotes

  1. Istalama includes the act of kissing the Stone, or wiping it if one is not able to kiss it, or gesturing towards it in greeting if neither of the above are possible. ↩
  2. Abū Yūsuf narrated it on p.98. ↩
