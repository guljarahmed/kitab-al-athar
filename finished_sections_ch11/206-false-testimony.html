206. FALSE TESTIMONY

644. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham ibn Abī’l-Haytham narrated to us from someone who narrated to him about Shurayḥ; he said, “When he caught a person in false testimony, if he was one of the people of the marketplace he sent someone along with him to the marketplace and said to the messenger, ‘Say to them, “Shurayḥ greets you and says, ‘We found this one out in false testimony so beware of him.’”’1756 If he was an Arab, a messenger is sent with him to his people’s mosque where the greatest number of them were gathered and the messenger was told to say something similar to what was said on the former occasion.”’”1757

Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah used to adhere to this, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, and did not think that he should be flogged,1758 but our verdict is that we think that along with that there should be a discretionary punishment which should not amount to forty lashes.”

645. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘A man narrated to me from ʿĀmir ash-Shaʿbī that he used to flog a person who bore false testimony with up to but not as many as forty lashes.’”1759

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this.”
