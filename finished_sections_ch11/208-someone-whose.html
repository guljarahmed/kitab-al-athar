208. SOMEONE WHOSE TESTIMONY IS NOT ACCEPTED BECAUSE HE IS A CLOSE RELATIVE OR FOR OTHER REASONS

648. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham narrated to us from Shurayḥ who said, “There are four people whose testimonies are not accepted on behalf of each other: the wife on behalf of her husband the husband on behalf of his wife, the father on behalf of his son and the son on behalf of his father, business partners on behalf of each other, and the testimony of someone punished for accusations of sexual impropriety.”’”1763

Muḥammad said, “We adhere to this and it is the verdict of Abū Ḥanīfah, may Allah, exalted is He, have mercy on him, except that we say the testimony of business partners for each other is accepted in matters outside of their partnership.”

649. Muḥammad said, “Abū Ḥanīfah informed us saying, ‘Al-Haytham narrated to us from ʿĀmir ash-Shaʿbī that he said, “A wife’s testimony on behalf of her husband is not accepted nor that of a husband on behalf of his wife, nor that of a father on behalf of his son nor that of a son on behalf of his father, nor that of business partners on behalf of each other, and Allah knows best.”’”
